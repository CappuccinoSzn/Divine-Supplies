About Divine Supplies Ltd

Who We Are

Divine Supplies Ltd is your comprehensive partner for a diverse range of products and services. We specialize in sales of church accessories and office supplies, alongside a full suite of professional services including graphic design, binding, lamination, driver's license renewal, passport photos, KRA and NTSA assistance, eulogies, banners, posters, printing, photocopying, scanning, branding, web development, and system design.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of services does Divine Supplies Ltd offer?

We offer a wide range of services including graphic design, binding, lamination, driver's license renewal, passport photos, KRA/NTSA assistance, eulogies, banners, posters, printing, photocopying, scanning, branding, web development, and system design.

Can you help with KRA and NTSA filings?

Yes, we provide assistance with various KRA (Kenya Revenue Authority) and NTSA (National Transport and Safety Authority) related services to make the process easier for you.

Do you offer custom web development and system design?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom websites and designing robust system solutions tailored to your specific business requirements and goals.

What kind of church accessories do you sell?

We supply a variety of church accessories including altar candles, communion sets, vestments, and other essential items for religious services.
